17 / 20
Risks and decisions
The chain needs boundaries

Three things
must be set early

Otherwise stronger tools only create faster rework.

Boundary 01

What is the master file?

Slides, sheets, Figma, or video may be the final deliverable. Pick the main file first.

Do not crown everything.
Boundary 02

Who owns the data?

Marketing analysis and spreadsheet models need human review, not blind trust in generated output.

Definitions beat charts.
Boundary 03

Who owns visual consistency?

Posters, videos, diagrams, and UI files should share one title system, palette, and story.

Style needs an owner.
